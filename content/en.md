# Building in the Era of Artificial Intelligence

Technology is changing at an unprecedented pace. The boundary between software engineering, product design, and artificial intelligence is dissolving.

I believe the developers of tomorrow are not just writing code — they are engineering workflows, coordinating autonomous agents, and building systems that think. This website serves as a central hub where I share my findings, building processes, courses, and experiments.

## The Philosophy

- **Minimalist & Fast**: Build lightweight products focused on user experience and fast iterations.
- **AI-Native**: Harness agentic AI and LLM workflows to accelerate engineering cycles.
- **Learn in Public**: Document every step of the journey, from initial ideas to production architecture.
